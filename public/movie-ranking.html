The Best Movies According to Jason

My top 4 movies of all time

Parasite

Directed by Bong Joon-ho.
This is simply the best written and visually represented film I have ever seen.

The French Dispatch

Directed by Wes Anderson.
You just never see visuals like Wes Anderson's, do you?

Get Out

Directed by Jordan Peele.
Creative idea and incredible story telling.

Mean Girls

Directed by Mark Waters.
My comfort movie. It's never a bad day to watch Mean Girls.
